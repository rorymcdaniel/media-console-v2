    - Left panel shows static current-input icon + label

  <how-to-verify>
    Deploy and run on the Pi 5 kiosk. Verify each requirement:

    **CAR-01 - Smooth rotation (no jank):**
    1. Tap the left panel to open the carousel
    2. Swipe horizontally on the carousel continuously for 5+ seconds in both directions
    3. Expected: Motion is smooth throughout — no stutters, freezes, or frame drops visible at the 1920x720 display refresh rate
    4. Also verify: Turn the physical encoder quickly (multiple detents) — no jank during rapid encoder input

    **CAR-02 - Natural snap deceleration:**
    1. Open the carousel and swipe with a medium-speed fling gesture, release mid-motion
    2. Expected: The carousel decelerates to the nearest card with a smooth curve — not instant (no abrupt stop), not elastic (no bounce past the card)
    3. Try at slow, medium, and fast fling speeds — snap should always feel physically natural

    **CAR-03 - 3D depth emphasis:**
    1. Open the carousel and look at all 6 visible cards
    2. Expected: The center card is clearly the largest and most opaque
    3. Expected: Cards immediately left and right of center are noticeably smaller and dimmer
    4. Expected: Cards at the far edges are the smallest and dimmest
    5. The visual hierarchy should make the selected input obvious at a glance

    **CAR-04 - 1:1 encoder response:**
    1. Close any open overlay
    2. Turn the physical input encoder exactly one detent
    3. Expected: Carousel opens (if hidden) and advances exactly one card — no lag between the physical turn and the visual step
    4. Turn back one detent — expected: carousel moves back one card immediately
    5. The response should feel instantaneous (sub-frame latency not perceptible)

    **Dismiss behavior:**
    6. Open the carousel by tapping the left panel
    7. Tap the dark backdrop (outside any card) — expected: carousel fades out, current input unchanged
    8. Open the carousel, wait 4+ seconds without interacting — expected: carousel auto-selects the focused card and closes

    **Left panel:**
    9. When the carousel is closed, verify the left panel shows the current active input's icon (large colored circle) and name label
    10. After changing input via the carousel, verify the left panel updates to show the newly selected input
  </how-to-verify>
